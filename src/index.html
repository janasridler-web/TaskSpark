Using TaskSpark in Offline Mode
You're about to use TaskSpark without a Google account. Here's what that means:
⚠ Please read before continuing All your tasks will be stored locally on this computer only. They cannot be accessed from another computer, and if you uninstall TaskSpark your tasks will be lost.
You can sign in with Google at any time from the sidebar to start syncing your tasks to Google Drive — making them available on any computer. Any tasks you've created in offline mode will be safely migrated when you connect.
